Journal of Animal, Plant and Social Science (JAPSS)

About Us

The Journal of Animal, Plant and Social Science (JAPSS) is an international peer-reviewed, online open access scientific journal, the source for a high quality paper in the fields of animal, plant and social sciences as a multidisciplinary including, Agricultural, Biotechnology, Environmental Sciences, Food Sciences, Medical Sciences, Library and Information Sciences, Social Sciences, Microbiology, Fisheries, Poultry Sciences, Anatomy, Histology, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, Virology, Pathology, Nutrition, Parasitology, Infectious Diseases, Veterinary Medicine, Wold History.

The aims of the JAPSS to offer an ordinary platform to all researchers and scientist to correspond and publishes original research papers, case report; reviews and short communications on the newest developments in the Animal, Plant and Social Science with strong importance on originality and scientific quality.
