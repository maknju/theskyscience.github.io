Author Guidelines

JAPSS is a bimonthly research journal that publishes original research articles, review articles, case reports and short communications in the field of Animal, Plant and Social Science. JAPSS allows abstracts 300 words and free unlimited access to full-text. The journal focuses on quick publication with services of, online research article tracking and email alert. When your manuscript is accepted for review with the understanding that the same work has not been published, that it is not under consideration for publication elsewhere, and that its submission for publication has been permitted by all authors and organization where the real work was approved out.

JAPSS publishes the following manuscript types:

  • - Original Research Articles
  • - Review Articles
  • - Short Communications
  • - Case Study

Plagiarism Detection

JAPSS verifies the originality of the submitted manuscript before publication. The manuscript originality check by Turnitin software. 16% similarity were allowed in the Journal of Animal, Plant and Social Science (JAPSS). Otherwise, the manuscript will be rejected automatically.

Manuscript Submission Format

  • - Title Page
  • - Abstract
  • - Keywords
  • - Introduction
  • - Materials and Methods
  • - Results and Discussion
  • - Conclusion
  • - Acknowledgements
  • - References

Title page:

(12ptTimes New Roman); Co-author1, Co-author1 ……Correspondence Author *,*1 Department, College, Country,2 Department, College, Country,*Correspondence Author: Name and email@yahoo.com or others

Abstract:

abstracts must be 300 words or not more than 300 words and consist of Background, Methods and Results, conclusion.

Keywords:

six keywords related to the research. For example, Animal; Breeding; Plant; Poultry Sciences;. . . Zoology.

Introduction:

The introduction is not more than 5 pages. The Introduction part discusses briefly and clearly, the problem investigated, with relevant references.

Materials and Methods:

Materials and Methods not more than 5 pages. It should be comprehensive adequate to permit experiments to be reproduced. All the procedures should be described in detail, formerly available procedures should be cited, and important modifications of the published trial should be mentioned briefly. Capitalize trade names and include the manufacturer's name and address. Sub-headings should be used. Methods in common use need not be described in detail.

Results and Discussion:

Results and Discussion not more than 5 pages. The results section should provide details of all of the experiments that are required to support the conclusions of the paper. The section may be divided into subsections, each with a concise subheading.

Conclusion:

The Conclusion must not exceed than one page. Main conclusion of the research must be a clear justification for their significance and relevance.

Acknowledgements:

Acknowledgements should be located in a separate section after the conclusion.

Funding:

If the study is supported by an external funding then it should be mentioned.

References:

The authors are responsible for the accuracy of the bibliographic information. It must be numbered successively in the classify that they are cited in the text for example [1], [1,2], [1,2,3]……[1, 30].

Journal references:

1. Shah AA, Liu Z, Qian C, Wu J, Zhong X (2019). (Title of article). J of Anim, Plant and Soci Sci. 27(1): 73-8. https://doi.org/10.3168/jds.2017-13901

Books:

2. Shah AA. (1984) Endophytic Bacillus megaterium colonization on structure strengthening, chemical composition and stabilization properties of Hybrid Pennisetum. New York: pp. 139–228

Websites:

3. Available from: www.isfqcbrazil.com.br/proceedings/2013/73.pdf
4. Available from: cn-polylysine.com/products/natural-antimicrobial/natamycin.html

Figures and Tables:

Must be uploaded in a separate file or at the end of the manuscript. The figures must be in jpg; jpeg or tif; tiff formats.

Short Communications:

same format.

Reviews Articles:

Review articles not more than 25 pages and contains complete reporting of significant literature. All formats are similar to those related to original papers. Review papers need not be separated into different sections such as Materials and methods, and Results and discussion, but should absolutely have an abstract and introduction.

Ethical Approval:

If the Authors use animals in the experiment then subjects in their research work and take ethical approval from the institute committee. The authors should be adding the “Ethical approval statement” in the material and methods section or before from acknowledgements section of the manuscript.

Publication Fee:

The author(s) can submit Processing fees by using International Debit or Credit Card and Paypal.

Author Guidelines

Author should read the submission instructions before submisstion of manuscript. Unformatted manuscripts will not be considered for publication.
